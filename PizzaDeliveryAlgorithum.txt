1. Decide what kind of pizza you want
2. Call
3. Place order 
  a. If paying over the phone, give CC information
  b. If paying with cash, tell them you will pay delivery person.
4. Wait
5. When delivery person arrives
  a. If you pay over the phone, take pizza from delivery person.
  b. If you are paying with cash, pay delivery person and take pizza.
6. Eat pizza
